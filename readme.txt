CONTROLS:
W- Jump
A- Left
D- Right

SPACE - shoot, you'll shoot down if you don't move
		      shoot right if you are moving right
		      shoot left if you are moving left

Wall Climb- you can climb walls if you are next to a wall and press left + up on the left wall or
								    right + up on the right wall

Goal: 
Don't touch the red block and shoot it till it dies!
